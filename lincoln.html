Facts about Lincoln

  • The capital city of Nebraska.
  • Population of 292,627 people.
  • Incorporated as a city on April 1st, 1869.
  • In the southeastern region of Nebraska.
  • Considered an urban area.
  • Has an average income of $34,433 compared to the rest of Nebraska's $33,127.
  • Named after Abraham Lincoln.
  • Is the origin of the Arbor Day Holiday.
  • The State Capitol building in Lincoln is a 400-foot tower with a statue of a man throwing seeds.
  • Home to the Lincoln Saltdogs, a professional baseball team.
  • Home to the world's largest porch swing, which is shaped like the state bird.
