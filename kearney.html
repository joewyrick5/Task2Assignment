Facts about Kearney

  • Fifth most populated city in Nebraska.
  • Population of 34,277 people.
  • Incorporated as a city on December 3, 1873.
  • In the south-central region of Nebraska.
  • Considered an urban area.
  • Has an average income of $26,793 compared to the rest of Nebraska's $33,127.
  • Originally named Fort Kearny after Colonel Stephen W. Kearny.
  • Located along the Platte River.
  • Home of the MONA, the Museum of Nebraska Art.
  • Annually hosts the spring migration of sandhill cranes, this brings in birdwatchers from around the world.
