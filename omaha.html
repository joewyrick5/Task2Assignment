Facts about Omaha

  • Most populated city in Nebraska.
  • Population of 485,153 people.
  • Incorporated as a city in 1857.
  • In the eastern region of Nebraska.
  • Considered an urban area.
  • Has an average income of $33,931 compared to the rest of Nebraska's $33,127.
  • Home of the Union Pacific Railroad, one of the largest railroad companies in the country.
  • Hometown of Warren Buffet.
  • Birthplace of civil rights leader Malcolm X, his childhood home is now a tourist attraction.
  • Has an annual rodeo and western themed celebration called River City Roundup.
  • Has an annual Omaha Film Festival for independent filmmakers from all over the world.
